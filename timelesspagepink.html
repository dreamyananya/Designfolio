About:

"Timeless Tales"is a mobile app that offers an immersive, vintage-themed exploration of historic architecture and forgotten landmarks.
Featuring interactive maps, AR restoration, architectural sketches, and immersive audio tours, it blends modern technology with nostalgic aesthetics, providing a rich, deep dive into history for architecture and culture enthusiasts.



Problem Statement:

Users like John, an architecture enthusiast, struggle with history apps that lack an immersive, vintage experience. Current apps use modern, sterile designs, missing the emotional connection users seek when exploring historic landmarks. John desires an app that blends historical exploration with a nostalgic aesthetic for a deeper engagement.



Project Background and Objective:

Timeless Tales seeks to provide an immersive, vintage-themed mobile app that brings forgotten landmarks and architecture to life. With vintage maps, AR restoration, architectural sketches, and curated storytelling, it combines nostalgic design with modern tools to enhance historical exploration and foster a deeper connection to the past.



Key takeaways from users prior to project accomplishment:

  • "The design is too modern, I was hoping for something that would make me feel like I’m truly stepping back in time."
  • "I wish there were more visual cues or storytelling that made me feel connected to the history I’m exploring."



Key takeaways from competitive audits:

  1. Google Arts & Culture:

    • Strength:

      • Global reach with a vast database of museums and landmarks.
      • High-quality visuals and integration with Google Street View.
      • Extensive educational content on history and art.
    • Weakness:

      • Lacks immersive, vintage aesthetics.
      • User interface feels more like an archive than an experience.
      • Not focused on architecture enthusiasts or historical site exploration.

  2. Historypin:

    • Strength:

      • User-generated content creates a community-driven experience.
      • Geolocation-based historical photos and stories.
      • Rich variety of historical content.
    • Weakness:
      • Lacks visual immersion and vintage design.
      • Content quality may be inconsistent.
      • No augmented reality or detailed guided tours.
